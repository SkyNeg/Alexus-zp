To get rid of the issue please follow these steps:

Log into your account control panel
Click on Manage for the Hosting Account that contains the domain name that you are working with.
Scroll down half way if necessary to click on the Frontpage icon
Click on the small Edit icon for the domain you are working with
In the second table, there are two boxes near the bottom of the settings page that should contain email addresses.
In both boxes please type in the exact email address that the contact form is supposed to go to.
Now you will click the Submit button and republish with Frontpage.
